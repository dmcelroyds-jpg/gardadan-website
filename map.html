Location Files

Ballybrackan Garda Station

Suspicion Rating: Medium

Small station, big kettle. Most cases begin with Dan misplacing a notebook.

Rumour: The filing cabinet knows more than Sergeant O’Dwyer.

Doyle’s Pub

Suspicion Rating: High

The unofficial headquarters of gossip, accidental confessions, and emergency chips.

Rumour: The clock once ran backwards and nobody paid their tabs for a week.

Ballybrackan GAA Club

Suspicion Rating: Loud

Home of county final chaos, missing footballs, tactical disasters, and shouting chairmen.

Rumour: The seagulls know where the spare balls are hidden.

Ballybrackan Harbour

Suspicion Rating: Foggy

Pretty during the day. Questionable after dark. Radio signals are not guaranteed to be friendly.

Rumour: Captain Byrne once argued with a buoy and lost.

The Old Lighthouse

Suspicion Rating: Very High

Abandoned, except when it mysteriously turns itself on during storms.

Rumour: If the light flashes three times, Doyle closes early.

Smuggler’s Cove

Suspicion Rating: Don’t Ask

Picturesque cliffs, hidden caves, and the strong feeling someone is watching from above.

Rumour: Tourists love it. Locals pretend it does not exist.

The Salt House

Suspicion Rating: Rotten

An abandoned fish factory that definitely should not have working lights at night.

Rumour: The smell is either old fish or old secrets.

Watch Hill

Suspicion Rating: Stormy

The best place to see strange lights, bad weather, and people pretending they are not scared.

Rumour: Mrs Flanagan saw something there in 1987 and refuses to elaborate.

The Tide Tunnel

Suspicion Rating: Extreme

Flooded, dangerous, and constantly involved in things Dan wishes he had not found.

Rumour: The tunnel goes further than the map admits.

Black Bog

Suspicion Rating: Screaming

Known for fog, strange noises, and locals confidently giving five different explanations.

Rumour: The bog does not take people. Usually.
