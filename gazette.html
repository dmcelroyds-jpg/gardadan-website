Main Story

Balls Found! County Final Saved!

Ballybrackan was thrown into sporting panic after every football at the GAA club vanished days before the county final. Garda Dan launched a full investigation, Gerard Downey wandered into several important clues, and the chairman continued shouting throughout.

The team went on to win the county final, though several locals insist the seagulls still “look guilty”.

Fog Probably Fine

Captain Byrne has warned locals not to trust the fog after 6pm. The council has responded by saying it is “monitoring the situation from indoors”.
