About Ballybrackan

Ballybrackan is a small Irish town where every mystery starts with gossip, every clue leads to Doyle’s Pub, and every seagull is at least mildly suspicious.

It is home to Garda Dan, traffic cone incidents, strange harbour lights, GAA chaos, and more secrets than one town should reasonably have.
