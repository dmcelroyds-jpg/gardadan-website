Welcome To The Ballybrackan Files

Every great comic town needs a cast of strange locals. Ballybrackan has more than most. Some are heroes. Some are suspects. Some are both before lunchtime.

Main Characters

GARDA DAN
Main HeroTea Powered

Garda Dan

Ballybrackan’s favourite Garda. Good-hearted, suspicious, easily distracted, and always trying to keep the peace while the town refuses to behave.

Threat Level: Mostly calm
Known Enemy: Seagulls, fog, paperwork
Last Seen: Investigating something ridiculous
“Right so… we’ll look into it after the tea.”
GERARD DOWNEY
Accidental DetectiveLucky

Gerard Downey

Gerard finds clues by wandering into the wrong places, overhearing gossip, and generally not knowing what is happening.

Threat Level: Confusing
Special Skill: Accidental evidence discovery
Last Seen: Following Dan by mistake
“Was I meant to find that?”
SEAMUS “SHOUTY” KELLY
GAA ChairmanVolume Warning

Seamus “Shouty” Kelly

The Ballybrackan GAA chairman. Believes all problems can be solved with tradition, shouting, and blaming the manager.

Threat Level: Loud
Known Enemy: Modern tactics
Last Seen: Shouting near the clubhouse
“THIS CLUB WAS BUILT ON DISCIPLINE!”
MARTY “THE TACTICIAN” MURPHY
Team ManagerTactics Board Owner

Marty “The Tactician” Murphy

The GAA manager who thinks every match can be won with arrows, diagrams, and yelling “more passion” at tired players.

Threat Level: Strategically unstable
Known Enemy: The chairman
Last Seen: Drawing impossible arrows
“You’re destroying the dressing room!”
PAUDIE CAN MAN
Suspicious LocalUnreliable Witness

Paudie Can Man

A local character who sometimes knows exactly what happened, but rarely knows why, when, or where he put it afterward.

Threat Level: Depends on the pub
Known Skill: Forgetting important details
Last Seen: Almost confessing
“In fairness, I was told to hide them.”
CAPTAIN BYRNE
Harbour LegendProbably Right

Captain Byrne

Old fisherman, storyteller, and sea-weather philosopher. Everyone thinks he’s exaggerating until it turns out he was right.

Threat Level: Cryptic
Known Enemy: Calm seas
Last Seen: Warning people too late
“The sea keeps better records than people.”
AOIFE
ResearcherToo Smart For This

Aoife

Sharp, brave, and organised. Aoife usually spots the clue everyone else has been standing beside for twenty minutes.

Threat Level: Clipboard dangerous
Known Enemy: Bad explanations
Last Seen: Correcting Dan’s theory
“That makes absolutely no sense, Dan.”
KAZ
Bin ManRocket Scientist?

Kaz

A calm, mysterious bin man who may or may not have once been a rocket scientist. He sees everything because nobody notices the bin man.

Threat Level: Quietly intelligent
Known Enemy: Bad engineering
Last Seen: Knowing too much
“Statistically inevitable.”
